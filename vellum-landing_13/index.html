Getting started

Up and running in 30 seconds.

Three quick steps — and one that really matters.

1

Add to Chrome

Install Vellum free from the Chrome Web Store. It works on both Claude and ChatGPT — no account or setup needed.

Important
2

Turn on dark mode

In Claude or ChatGPT settings, set appearance to Dark. Vellum's themes are crafted for dark mode — on light mode they'll look washed out.

3

Pick a theme

Click the Vellum icon in your toolbar, choose any theme, and it applies instantly across the whole interface.

🌙

Why dark mode? Vellum's animated backgrounds and palettes are designed to glow against a dark interface. If a theme looks faded, you're still in light mode — switch your AI app to Dark in its appearance settings and it'll come alive.
