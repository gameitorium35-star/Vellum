Privacy Policy

Last updated May 2026

Summary

Vellum does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.

What data does Vellum access?

Vellum injects CSS and a canvas animation into claude.ai and chatgpt.com to apply your chosen theme. It does not read, modify, or transmit any conversation content, personal information, or browsing activity.

What is stored locally?

Your selected theme, Pro status, and license key (if applicable) are saved using Chrome's built-in sync storage. This data stays within your browser and syncs across your devices through your Google account. Vellum has no server and cannot access this data.

License validation

When you activate a Pro license key, Vellum sends the key to Polar's API (api.polar.sh) to verify it is valid. This is the only external network request the extension makes. No personal data is included in this request.

Analytics and tracking

None. No analytics, no telemetry, no cookies, no fingerprinting, no tracking scripts of any kind.

Third parties

The only external service involved is Polar for payment processing and license validation, governed by their own privacy policy. Vellum does not share data with any other third party.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. Vellum will never introduce data collection without clear notice to users.

Contact

Questions can be sent to supportvellumapp@gmail.com.
